Q. Match the columns

B: 1-A, 2-B, 3-C, 4-D

C: 4-A, 3-B, 2-D, 1-C

D: 4-C, 3-C, 2-A, 1-B

Answer:Option1

Explaination:Option 1 is the answer. col-lg-12 - This means that on the screen size 1200px and more, col-md-12 - This means that on the screen size between 1200px to 992px, col-sm-12 - This means that on the screen size between 992px to 768px, col-xs-12 - This means that on the screen size between 786px to 0px.

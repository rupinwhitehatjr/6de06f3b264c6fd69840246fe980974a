Q. Which of the following is correct about Accelerometer component in thunkable?

Which of the following is correct about Accelerometer component in thunkable?image

A: It is used to measure the force of acceleration acting on the phone

B: The values obtained from the sensor are in metres per second squared

C: Both are correct

D: Both are wrong.

Answer:Option 3

Explaination:Option 3 is the answer. The accelerometer sensor is used to measure the force of acceleration acting on the phone. The sensor reports this force for each of the three principal axes: the x axis, y axis and, the z axis. The values obtained from the sensor are in metres per second squared.

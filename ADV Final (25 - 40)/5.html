Q. The unicorn just saw a rainbow and it is in a very happy mood. What do you think the unicorn will say ?

A: wow

B: sad sad

C: happy happy

D: angry

Answer:Option 3 (text)

Explaination:Option 3 is the answer. In this the mood of actor1 is already set to “happy” and which is not changing anywhere. So when run actor 1 will say “happy happy” as mentioned.

Q. What are the steps for deleting an Agent in a Dialogflow?

What are the steps for deleting an Agent in a Dialogflow?image

A: Click on “View all agents” -> Select “Chabot” -> Click on “Gear” icon -> click on “DELETE THIS AGENT”

B: Click on “Intents” -> Click on “Gear” icon -> click on “DELETE THIS AGENT”

C: Click on “Integrations” -> Go to “Default Welcome Intent” -> click on “DELETE THIS AGENT”

D: Click on “Intents” -> Go to “Default Welcome Intent” -> click on “DELETE THIS AGENT”

Answer:Option 2 (image)

Explaination:To delete a chatbot which is created use following procedure, Go to ‘view all agents’ then select your chatbot after that click on gear icon and then scroll down and you will see option to delete agent.

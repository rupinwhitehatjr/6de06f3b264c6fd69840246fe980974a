Q. Disha and her sister are playing the bottle spin game . The random number chosen by the computer is 5. Can you guess in which direction the bottle will point.

A: The program throw an error.

B: The program will not show any thing as an output because there is no IF condition when random number is 5.

C: The Bottle will point left or right.

D: The Bottle will point towards Right.

Answer:Option 2 (text)

Explaination:Option 2 is the answer. When ‘var x’ is “5” program will not stop. But it will not display anything on the screen. Because we are checking condition for (x<5) or (x>5).

Q. In which direction will the bottle point when the random number is 70 ?

A: Right

B: Top

C: Left

D: Bottom

Answer:Option 1 (text)

Explaination:Option 1 is the answer. When value of random number is 70, which comes in range of (50- 75) it will turn to right direction. As mention in given code “if(y>50 && y<75)” bottle will turn to right.

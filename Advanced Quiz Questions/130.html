Q. Identify the possible values for "x" , to show element "LEFT"

A: 1,2,3,4

B: 1,2,3,4,5

C: 0,1,2,3,4,5

D: 0,1,2,3,4

Answer:Option 2 (text)

Explaination:Option 2 is the answer. To show element “LEFT” it should satisfy condition “if(x<=5))”. That means x should be (1/2/3/4/5).

Q. Identify output of given code.

A: It will draw a circle

B: It will draw a triangle

C: It will draw a rectangle

D: It will draw a cube

Answer:option 2(text)

Explaination:Option 2 is the answer. The code mention in question will draw a triangle. It start drawing after pen is turned right for 90 degree. Then it will draw a line of 200 pixels first, then it will turn left to 120 degree and again will draw a line of 200 pxls. And at last it will turn left 120 degree again and will draw a line of 200 pxls and will stop.

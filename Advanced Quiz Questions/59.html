Q. What will be the Correct output of the Given Code when the button "playButton" is clicked?

A: option 1 is "mainScreen"

B: option 2 is showing "playScreen"

C: Both A and B

D: None of the above

Answer:Option B (image)

Explaination:Option 2 is the answer. When “playButton” is clicked the device will change its screen to “playScreen” as shown in option2 because it will execute setScreen(“playScreen”) block. And on “playScreen”, "buttonCube" and "labelBlank" components are placed which shows a cube shaped button and label to display score.

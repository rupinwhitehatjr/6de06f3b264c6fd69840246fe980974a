Q. Find the correct output for the following code ?

A: If the score reaches 5 points, the speed decreases by 5. Else if the score reaches 10 points, the speed will increase by 10

B: If the score reaches 5 points, the speed increases by 5. Else if the score reaches 10 points, the speed will increase by 10

C: If the score reaches 5 points, the speed increases by 5. Else if score reaches 10 points, the speed will decrease by 10

D: If score reaches 5 points, the speed decreases by 5. Else if score reaches 10 points, the speed will decrease by 10.

Answer:Option 2 (text)

Explaination:If block and else if block both are used to update the score variable so, if the score reaches 5 points, the speed increases by 5. and if (else if) the score reaches 10 points, the speed will increase by 10.

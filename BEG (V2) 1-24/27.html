Question Number: 2 in class 6 of BEG-V2

Q. As you can see we have the numbers placed on the grid, Find out correct grid coordinates for Number 6.

A: Number 6 is at (3,4)

B: Number 6 is at (4,3)

C: Number 6 is at (4,2)

D: Number 6 is at (3,2)

Answer:Option 2 (text)

Explaination:X coordinate is the horizontal value and Y coordinate is the vertical value. This is how we read positions on a grid as x,y First value is always x and second is y. For x, we go right and for y, we go up. So, we always follow the sequence: Go RIGHT as much specified and then go UP to arrive at the x,y location Number 6 at (4,3). The grid location corresponds to (x,y) = 222,176 in Sprite Lab.

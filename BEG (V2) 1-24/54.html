Q. Which is the best algorithm for calling a friend on the telephone?

Which is the best algorithm for calling a friend on the telephone?image

A: Step 1: Talk to the friend Step 2: Pick up phone Step 3: Dial a number Step 4: No answer, hang up Step 5: Hang up

B: Step1: Pick up the phone Step 2: Dial the number or find the number if saved Step 3: If busy hang up , Wait for 5 minutes and repeat step 2 Step 4: If answering machine, leave message and hang up Step 5: If no one answers, hang up Step 6: If answered , TALK to friend Step 7: Hang up

C: Step 1: Pick up the phone Step 2: talk to friend Step 3: dial a number Step 4: listen for dial tone Step 5: hang up

D: Step 1: Call a friend Step 2: use the phone Step 3: Talk to friend

Answer:Option 2 (text)

Explaination:Option 2 explains explores all the possibilities we might face while calling a friend hence it is the correct answer.

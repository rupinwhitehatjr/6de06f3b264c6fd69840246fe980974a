Q. Swimming right and left will make the hippos go back and forth horizontally from East to West and back again. How will change movement direction affect the code?

A: No change. The hippo will continue moving back and forth in a straight line

B: Since the code is outside the behaviour it will not affect the behaviour. The hippo will continue back forth (east-to-west-and back again) in a straight line

C: Hippos cannot be bothered with any behaviours. They are wild animals

D: The hippo direction will be titled as it moves . It will still be going back forth but at a slightly tilted angle bending South east. Moving between south east and north west directions.

Answer:Option 4 (text)

Explaination:The hippo direction will be titled as it moves . It will still be going back forth but at a slightly tilted angle bending South east. Moving between south east and north west directions.

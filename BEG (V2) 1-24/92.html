Q. When Up arrow is pressed continuously which car would move faster?

A: Red Car

B: Blue Car

C: Both red and blue car will move at the same speed.

D: No one will move

Answer:Option 1 (text)

Explaination:The "While key press event," will run the code over and over again until the key stops being pressed. So the red car will move ahead as long as the up key is pressed. But the blue car will only move once, since it is controlled by the "when" key press event.

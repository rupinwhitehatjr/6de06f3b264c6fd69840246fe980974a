Q. What happens when Alice drinks the Magical Potion (drink me)?

A: Alice grows big

B: Alice shrinks

C: Alice will move

D: none of the above

Answer:Option 2 (text)

Explaination:Alice shrinks because we have used variable to set the "size" of alice, and we reduced the value of the "size" block thus Alice shrunk.

Q. What happens when Alice enters Wonderland and touches the Cup Cake (eat me) ?

A: Alice changes colour

B: Alice shrinks

C: Alice grows big

D: Alice will move

Answer:Option 3 (text)

Explaination:We have used the variable "size" to set the height of the Alice sprite. We increased the value of the variable size hence Alice grew in size.

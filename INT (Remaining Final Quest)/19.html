Q. when the ball hits the pink paddle, in which direction should the ball move?

A: North west

B: South east

C: North east

D: South west

Answer:Option 3 (text)

Explaination:The ball should move upward and the right side as well when it touches the pink handle, So for this behavior we need to move the ball both, in the north and east directions.

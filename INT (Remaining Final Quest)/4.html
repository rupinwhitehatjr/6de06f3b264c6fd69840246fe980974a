Q. In the soccer activity, what is the formula for pixels to move the Soccer ball towards the Boy 2 Sprite?

A: Y of soccer ball – Y of boy 2

B: X of boy 2 – X of soccer ball

C: Y of boy 2 – Y of soccer ball

D: X of soccer ball – X of boy 2

Answer:Option B (Text)

Explaination:We want to pass/move the ball sprite towards the boy 2 sprite, so we will need to match the X position of the boy 2 sprite and the ball sprite, as X position is used for EAST/WEST direction

Q. Harry wants to drink from the can. If he moves towards the can, which option correctly describes the change of x and y position of Harry?

A: X and Y position of Harry remain the same

B: X position of Harry decreases and Y position remains same.

C: X position of Harry increases and Y position remains the same

D: Both X and Y position of Harry increase

Answer:Option 3 (text)

Explaination:Only the X position of Harry should increase, as harry is moving horizontally, towards the right.

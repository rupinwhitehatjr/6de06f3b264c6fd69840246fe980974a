Q. How do we assign the y position to sprite object called "lucile" according to mouse movement?

How do we assign the y position to sprite object called "lucile" according to mouse movement?image

A: lucile.y=World.mousey;

B: lucile.y=World.mouseY;

C: lucile.Y=mousey;

D: lucile.Y=World.mouseY;

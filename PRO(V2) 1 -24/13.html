Q. We want to move our ball object towards the block, can you help us choose the correct option for the same?

A: ball.velocityY = -2; ball.velocityX = 0;

B: ball.velocityY = -2; ball.velocityX = 2;

C: ball.velocityY = 0; ball.velocityX = 2;

D: ball.velocityY = 0; ball.velocityX = -2;

Answer:Option 3 (text)

Explaination:For moving objects to the right we set velocity by giving velocity in x direction as positive and velocity in y direction as zero.
